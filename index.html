Aplazo's Silent Churn

A significant share of approved users never reach their second transaction. Credit is approved, limit assigned — but real activation never happens. This means direct GMV loss and wasted CAC from acquisition spend.

Why do they leave without buying?
Onboarding friction: Users can't find a merchant at the moment of approval.
Generic messaging: Communications not tailored to user history or behavior.
Wrong timing: Campaigns miss the user's actual purchase intent windows.
No smart nudges: No system detects early abandonment signals before users churn.
Priority segments
Approved — no first purchaseHigh
1 purchase, inactive 30+ daysHigh
2–3 purchases, inactive 60+ daysMedium
Frequent actives (retain)Low
How does ActivaBot work?

It combines propensity models, real-time behavioral signals and omnichannel messaging to reach the right user, with the right message, at the exact right moment. BigQuery + CleverTap + Claude API orchestrate fully automated reactivation journeys.

Propensity Model

Predicts which users are most likely to reactivate in the next 48h using BigQuery behavioral signals.

Hyper-personalized Messages

Claude API generates dynamic copy per segment: history, available limit, nearby merchants and personalized offers.

Automatic Omnichannel

Push, email, SMS and in-app orchestrated via CleverTap Journeys based on each user's preferred channel.

DND Frequency Control

Smart system that prevents message overload and protects the Aplazo brand experience.

Results Dashboard

Real-time metrics: reactivation rate, CTR, conversion and GMV generated by the bot.

Continuous Learning

The model feeds back from every interaction to improve predictions week over week.
