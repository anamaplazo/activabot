The Solution

ActivaBot

📡
Pillar 01
Open the channel
A new path to signup for users who don't finish in 5 minutes. We reach out — they don't have to come back on their own.
Pillar 02
Proactive follow-up
If you get stuck, we follow up on WhatsApp. Not reactive — we detect the drop and reach out first.
🛠️
Pillar 03
Give agents the tools
Not just informational — the bot walks through each step with the user. Real tools: ID verification, real-time data.
