The Problem

The digital channel leaves users alone

Unlike a physical store, in digital there's nobody walking the user through registration. The result: massive losses at two critical funnel points — without the product ever failing.

Where the funnel drops (no rejections on our end)
01
Started → KYC Evaluation: −32,762 users/month (−25%)
Users abandon before attempting anything. Zero technical friction. These are the two moments where the product doesn't fail — and it's not a rejection.
Why do they leave? The evidence
02
Card Barrier Study (n=92)
26% don't understand why their data is being requested. 22% fear fraud. "I'll do it later" is an escape mechanism — not a lack of time.
03
Support tickets: 21,391/month
19.9% of total support volume are product understanding questions — users who arrived without knowing what Aplazo is.
04
Central hypothesis
It's not technical friction — it's a lack of intent and guidance.
Proof that guidance works
9x
Registration completion
Users with full informational guidance complete at 36.26% vs 3.97% organic — 9x more.
6x
Ambassador storefronts
Storefronts with an ambassador: 6.1 new customers/store/month vs 1.0 in BAU — 6x more conversion.
The mechanism is the same: someone who explains, builds trust and accompanies at the right moment.
The scale problem
01
Only 10% of users contact support during registration
The vast majority struggle alone — without asking for help.
02
Ambassadors only operate in physical stores
The most effective conversion tool doesn't exist in the digital channel.
03
~124,000 users/month get stuck in signup with no contact
Nobody reaches out. No nudge, no guidance, no recovery.
The solution: proactive AI assistant
Replicate the ambassador role in the digital channel

Proactive, on WhatsApp — educates before asking for data and accompanies throughout the entire process. Not reactive: if the user gets stuck, we follow up and guide them to complete their registration.
