Our Hypothesis

It's not technical friction —
it's a lack of intent & guidance

The data points to one clear pattern: users who receive guidance complete at dramatically higher rates. The problem is behavioral, not technical.

The solution: proactive AI assistant
Replicate the ambassador role in the digital channel

Proactive, on WhatsApp — educates before asking for data and accompanies throughout the entire process. Not reactive: if the user gets stuck, we follow up and guide them to complete their registration.

The mechanism is the same: someone who explains, builds trust and accompanies at the right moment.
